Montfort High School,Repalle

Our History

Montfort High School, Repalle, Guntur was established in the year 2000, June. Montfort Brothers of St. Gabriel began this school with 24 student and 650 students are studying in the school at present. Bro. Kuriakose, Bro.Berchamans Joseph, Bro.Thomas Reddy and Bro. Sudhakar Reddy, Bro. Shine Alex and Bro.Bala Reddy served this institution as Directors of this institution. Rev.Bro.Jose Emmanuel is the present Correspondent and Principal of this institution assisted by vice Principal Rev. Bro.Chinnappa Reddy and dedicated, committed teachers and staff. The school has achieved 100% result with all distinctions in the S.S.C board examinations continuously.
